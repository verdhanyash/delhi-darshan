About Delhi

Delhi, the capital of India, is a city of contrasts - where ancient history meets modern dynamism. Spanning over thousands of years, Delhi has been a witness to the rise and fall of several empires. From the historic Red Fort to the modern metro system, the city seamlessly blends its rich past with a promising future.
